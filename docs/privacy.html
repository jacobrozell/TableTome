Overview

Tabletome is an offline-first iOS app for tabletop battle games (Age of Sigmar: Spearhead and Warhammer 40,000 11th Edition in v1.0). You do not need an account. This policy describes what the app stores on your device and when it uses the network.

Data stored on your device

  • Guide progress — optional checkmarks for Getting Started steps.
  • Guided Match state — army selections, battle tracker, phases, and scores for in-progress games.
  • Match history — saved match summaries you choose to keep.
  • Collection (Models) — armies, units, painting pipeline stages, tags, and optional unit photos you add.
  • Appearance and preferences — theme, onboarding completion, and in-app tips you have seen.
  • Backup files — if you export or import collection data, those files are created or read at your direction.

All of the above stays on your iPhone or iPad unless you explicitly share it (for example, exporting a backup file or a match paste code).

Data we do not collect

  • No analytics or crash reporting in v1.0.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No developer-operated account or cloud profile storage.
  • No sale of personal information.

Network use

  • Offline core — guides, rules reference, and Guided Match work without internet after install.
  • Settings links — Privacy, Support, Accessibility, and source repository open in Safari when you tap them.
  • Official publisher links — optional links on game guide screens open externally (for example, Warhammer Community downloads).
  • Nearby match sync (optional) — when both players choose to sync a Guided Match, the app may use local Wi‑Fi or Bluetooth between nearby devices. Match state is exchanged peer-to-peer; it is not uploaded to a Tabletome server. iOS may show a local network permission prompt the first time you host or join.

Your choices

  • Replay the app tour or battle-tracker tips from Settings.
  • Export or import collection data from Settings → Collection & Data.
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data.

Children

Tabletome is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as features change. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect revisions.
