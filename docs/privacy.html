Overview

Tabletome is an offline-first iOS app. You do not need an account to use it. This policy describes what information the app handles on your device in v0.1.

Data we store on your device

  • Guide progress — optional checkmarks for Getting Started steps, stored in iOS UserDefaults on your device.
  • Appearance preference — your light / dark / system theme choice, stored locally.

Data we do not collect

  • No analytics or crash reporting in v0.1.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No server-side account or profile storage.
  • No sale of personal information.

Network use

Core guide and rules content ships inside the app and works offline. Settings links (privacy, support, accessibility, tip jar) open in Safari when you tap them. Official Warhammer PDF links on game guide screens also open externally.

Your choices

You can clear guide progress anytime in Settings → Reset Guide Progress. Deleting the app removes all locally stored app data.

Children

Tabletome is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as features change. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect revisions.
